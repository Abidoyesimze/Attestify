# Breakpoints Used

- `sm`: 640px
- `md`: 768px
- `lg`: 1024px
- `xl`: 1280px
- `2xl`: 1536px

Notes:
- Sections use `.section` helper with padding scaling at 640px and 1024px.
- Grids generally stack to 1-col on <640px, 2-col at md, 3-col at lg.
- Navbar wallet/desktop links show from md and up; mobile menu below md.
